📋 FIR Report

If your item was stolen or still missing after a long time, file an FIR at your nearest police station. Track your report here.

📌 How to file an FIR

  1. Visit your nearest police station (or local campus security).
  2. Carry your college ID and describe the item.
  3. Get a copy of the FIR with the FIR number.
  4. Come back here and update your report with the FIR number.
