How It Works

01

Post Your Item

Lost something? Post it with description, photo, and location. Found something? Post where and what you found.

02

Get Matched

Anyone who spots a match sends a match request. The owner gets notified instantly.

03

Verify & Reunite

The owner confirms the match. Both parties connect and the item is returned safely.

04

QR Stickers

Stick a QR tag on your belongings. If found, the finder scans it and reaches you directly.
